🔬 Research Interests / 研究方向

My research lies at the intersection of machine learning and dynamical systems, with a focus on data-driven methods for modeling stochastic nonlinear dynamics, sequential Bayesian filtering, and the theoretical analysis of deep Transformers.

研究领域横跨机器学习与动力系统，专注于随机非线性动力系统的数据驱动建模、序列贝叶斯滤波，以及深度 Transformer 的理论分析。

  • Kalman Filter — Bayesian state estimation for sequential data / 序列数据的贝叶斯状态估计
  • Koopman Operator Theory — Linear representations of nonlinear dynamics / 非线性动力系统的线性提升
  • Transformer & LLM — Theoretical analysis of deep attention networks; mitigating over-smoothing via spectral regularization / 深度注意力网络的理论分析；基于谱正则化缓解过平滑问题
